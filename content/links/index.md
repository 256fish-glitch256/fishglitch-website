## Indie Web

### Smol web
- **Tildeverse < ~ > (https://tildeverse.org/)** - join a small community of people hanging out on IRC like the old days and much more!
- **Wiby Search (https://www.wiby.org/)** - search engine for the Classic Web
- **Infinite Mac (https://infinitemac.org/)** - online Virtual Machines of old MacOSes! Cool thing is you can download files that you create onto your computer!
- **kr1sp1n/awesome-gemini (https://github.com/kr1sp1n/awesome-gemini)** - Cool links regarding Gemini protocol
- **ssshake/retro-computing-internet-resources (https://github.com/ssshake/retro-computing-internet-resources)** - even more resources regarding retro web
